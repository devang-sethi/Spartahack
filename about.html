About Our Team!

We are a team of four college students with a shared enthusiasm for technology and a knack for problem-solving through code. While navigating our academic careers, we identified a common challenge among students: the lack of a streamlined way to access and offer peer-to-peer services on campus. This challenge inspired us to create a platform that connects students with a range of services like haircuts, baked goods, used book sales, and tech exchanges. Our project is designed to harness the diversity of skills within the student body, facilitating a community-driven marketplace. By doing so, we aim to enhance the campus experience, foster a supportive network, and promote a sustainable cycle of resource sharing. As we continue to develop our project, we remain committed to refining our craft and contributing to a more interconnected student community.
